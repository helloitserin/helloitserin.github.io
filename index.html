Here are the instructions to the different games.

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple game where you have three choices: rock, paper and scissors. Rock wins scissors, Scissors wins paper and Paper wins rock. If the computer and you use the same symbol, it is a tie. In this situation, no points are added.

Whack-a-thing is Whack-a-mole without the moles. In this game, you use you finger to whack the items.
